PIG DICE GAME

PIG DICE GAME RULES:

OBJECTIVES

The objective of Pig Dice Game is to score 100 points before the opponent does. The Pig Game is played with a single six sided dice.

GAMEPLAY

In each turn a player rolls a dice repeatedly until either 1 is rolled or a player decides to hold and pass on the turn to it's opponent.

The number player rolls is added to a temporary score. If the player rolls a 1, they lose their temporary score and turn is passed to their opponent.

If the player gets any other number, they can decide to continue and roll the dice.

If the player decides to hold, then their turn total(temporary score) is added to the overall score.

The first player to score 100 points wins the game.
